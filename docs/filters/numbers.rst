============
NumberFilter
============

Filter numbers. It allows complex filter like:


.. list-table:: Operators
   :widths: 30 70

   * - 100
     - equals 100
   * - =100
     - equals 100
   * - > 100
     - greater than 100
   * - < 100
     - lower than 100
   * - >= 100
     - greater or equal than 100
   * - >= 100
     - lower or equal than 100
   * - 100..200
     - betwenen 100 and 200
   * - <> 100
     - not equal to 100
   * -  1,4,5
     - list of numbers
